Cheesecake Recipe

Description

This is the recipe for the famous new york cheesecake created by me :)

Ingredients

  • Cream-cheese
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Sour cream

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 F
  2. Process the crust ingredients
  3. Press into a springform pan
  4. bake
  5. Triple wrap the pan in heavy duty foil
